Access to Nara from Kansai International Airport

Airport Limousine Bus

This is the simplest way of getting to the Nara venue area and hotels.
It is highly recommended for first time visitors to Japan.

Direct bus service to Nara area hotels leave roughly once an hour from Bus Stop No. 9 on the 1F Arrivals area of Kansai International Airport.

Fee (one-way): 1,800 JPY; Travel Time: 80-90 min.
